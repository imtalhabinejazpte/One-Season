FAQ

Good questions, straight answers

What happens during a tune-up?
We inspect, test, and clean the key parts of your heating or cooling system — refrigerant, electrical connections, airflow, and safety controls — so it runs efficiently and reliably all season.
How often will you visit?
Twice a year — once in spring for your AC, once in fall for your heat. We schedule both and send reminders so you never miss one.
What is the Rainy Day Fund?
Every month of membership earns credit toward a future system replacement — so when it's time to upgrade, you've already got a head start.
Is there a contract? Can I cancel?
No long-term contracts. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime — no penalties, no hassle.
Do you service all brands?
Yes. Our licensed technicians service and maintain all major makes and models of heating and air conditioning equipment.
How do the repair discounts work?
Members save on every repair — 5% on Premium, 15% on Ultimate — plus lower diagnostic fees and priority scheduling whenever something needs attention.
